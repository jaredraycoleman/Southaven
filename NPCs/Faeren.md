- *Domains:* Nature, Growth, Cycles
- *Symbol:* A tree with entwined antlers
- *Personality:* Faeren loved the world they created but initially viewed mortals as invaders. Over time, he came to respect their adaptability, though he remains neutral toward their struggles. He is worshipped by druids, hunters, and those who live in harmony with nature.
