The Marble Academy is a prestigious institution of higher learning that serves as the intellectual heart of Lusteris. Founded centuries ago by a group of scholars and mages, the academy is dedicated to the pursuit of knowledge, research, and magical studies. The academy is housed in a grand marble building that serves as a beacon of learning and enlightenment in the city. The academy offers a wide range of courses and programs, covering everything from history and philosophy to alchemy and spellcasting. The academy is home to a vast library that contains rare tomes, ancient scrolls, and magical artifacts. The academy is governed by a council of scholars, mages, and administrators who oversee its operations and ensure that its traditions are upheld.
