The Duskrunners are a notorious criminal guild operating out of [Lusteris](Lusteris.md), controlling smuggling routes and engaging in various illicit activities across the region. Known for their efficiency and secrecy, they have become a dominant force in the criminal underworld, dealing in stolen goods, contraband transport, and high-stakes espionage. 

The Duskrunners have a network of hidden routes and safe houses, allowing them to move unseen and strike without warning. There are whispers of alliances with dangerous factions, though few know the extent of these dealings.
