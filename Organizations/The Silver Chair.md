The Silver Chair represents the nobility and aristocracy of Lusteris, serving as the political and social elite of the city. The guild is made up of wealthy landowners, influential merchants, and powerful politicians who wield considerable influence over the city's affairs. The guild is named after the grand silver chair that serves as the symbol of its authority. The guild elects a ruler known as the High Lord, who represents Lusteris in matters of state and diplomacy. The High Lord is chosen from among the noble families of the city and serves for life or until they are deposed.
