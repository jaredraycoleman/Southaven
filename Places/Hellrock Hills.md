## Description

The Hellrock Hills rise west of [[Southaven]] like a labyrinth of stone, a sprawling maze of towering brown Metamorphosed volcanic rock and biotite monzogranite formations weathered into twisted spires, narrow passages, and hidden canyons. The hills earned their name from the ruddy, iron-rich stone that glows like smoldering embers in the light of dawn and dusk, and from the treacherous paths that wind through their depthsâ€”easy to enter, difficult to escape. Wind howls through the stone corridors with an eerie wail, and the terrain shifts from sun-baked plateaus to shadowed ravines where even experienced scouts can lose their way.

The Hellrock Hills have long been a haven for bandits and outlaws who use the natural fortifications to establish camps and hideouts. Deep within the maze of stone lies Fort Hellrock, a ruined fortress that [[Varnek the Scarred]] has transformed into his stronghold, coordinating the bandit forces that now emerge from the hills to raid the countryside and blockade [[Southaven]].

## DM Notes
- Inspired by Alabama Hills - a maze of rounded boulders and rock formations, but much larger scale
- Multiple bandit camps throughout, but Fort Hellrock is the main stronghold
- The party will need to navigate the confusing terrain while dealing with patrols and ambushes
- The natural maze makes it easy for bandits to escape and regroup
