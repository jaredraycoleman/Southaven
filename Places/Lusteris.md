Lusteris is a bustling, cosmopolitan city-state located at the edge of the Deadlands, the vast desert that stretches east of the Pale Mountains. As the only major settlement in the region, Lusteris serves as a melting pot of cultures, religions, and traditions. The city is known for its vibrant nightlife, bustling markets, and thriving underworld activities. Despite its harsh desert surroundings, Lusteris is a hub of commerce, culture, and intrigue, attracting travelers, traders, and adventurers from all corners of the world.

![[Lusteris_97da916c.webp]]

The city is ruled by a council of guilds, each representing a different aspect of society. The guilds are:
- **The Marble Academy**: a prestigious institution of higher learning that serves as the intellectual heart of Lusteris. The academy is dedicated to the pursuit of knowledge, research, and magical studies. The academy is governed by a council of scholars, mages, and administrators who oversee its operations and ensure that its traditions are upheld.
- **The Silver Chair**: represents the nobility and aristocracy of Lusteris, serving as the political and social elite of the city. The guild is made up of wealthy landowners, influential merchants, and powerful politicians who wield considerable influence over the city's affairs.
- **The Sapphire Temple**: the spiritual center of Lusteris, serving as a place of worship, healing, and divine guidance. The temple is not dedicated to a single deity but rather to a pantheon of gods and goddesses who are revered by the people of the city.
- **The Obsidian Army**: the military force of Lusteris, responsible for the defense and security of the city. The army is made up of professional soldiers, mercenaries, and adventurers who are trained to protect the city from external threats.
- **The Emerald Bank**: the economic powerhouse of Lusteris, serving as the financial heart of the city. The bank is responsible for managing the city's wealth, trade, taxation, and commerce.

Each guild has its own power and influence, and they often compete and cooperate with each other to achieve their goals. The council of guilds meets regularly to discuss matters of state, make decisions, and resolve disputes. 

There are many other smaller guilds in Lusteris, each representing a different trade, craft, or profession. These guilds have less power and influence than the council of guilds but still play an important role in the city's economy and society. There are also many criminal guilds operating in Lusteris, such as:
- [[Black Thorns]]: a bandit faction known for stealing from the rich and distributing their spoils among the poor. They often clash with the Duskrunners over territory. The Black Thorns see themselves as champions of the downtrodden, while the Duskrunners view them as reckless idealists.
- [[Duskrunners]]: a ruthless and efficient guild that specializing in smuggling contraband between Lusteris and other cities including Southaven, Solara, and Seawatch.

## Description

Lusteris is a bustling, cosmopolitan city-state located in the heart of the Deadlands, the vast desert that stretches east of the Pale Mountains. As the only major settlement in the region, Lusteris serves as a melting pot of cultures, religions. Just like a modern city, there are skyscrapers, neon lights (powered by magic), and bustling streets. The city is divided into districts, each with its own unique character and purpose. The city is known for its vibrant nightlife, bustling markets, and thriving underworld activities. Despite its harsh desert surroundings, Lusteris is a hub of commerce, culture, and intrigue, attracting travelers, traders, and adventurers from all corners of the world.
