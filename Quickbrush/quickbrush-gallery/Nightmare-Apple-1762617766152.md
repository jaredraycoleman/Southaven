## Original Description

These apples grow on trees tainted by corruption. They appear glossy and red with faint violet veining beneath the skin, and they smell oddly sweet, almost intoxicating.

Effects (When Eaten)

Nightmare Curse: Within 1d4 hours, the eater must succeed on a DC 15 Wisdom saving throw or suffer disturbing visions when next they sleep.
